Haiti, the Carribean

47% of the Haiti population suffer from Malnutrition. Haiti currently stands as the poorest country in the Western Hemisphere with half of the population earning an average of a dollar each day. Hunger and malnutrition are widespread problems that continuously gets worse instead of seeing much improvement.

The major contributor that is only making the situation of malnutrition in Haiti worse is the Haitian government which is highly unstable and causes disorder in the country. The government is letting foreign corporations pay Haitian citizens inadequate work wages and allows the bankruptcy of farmers who in turn lose everything.
